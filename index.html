Slack Export Prettify

The goal of this application is to present a chat history export from Slack in a human readable format. To get started, follow these instructions:

  1. Download your Slack team's history. This can be done by navigating to https://$YOUR_TEAM_NAME.com/services/export. The export will come in the form of a zip file containing many directories (channels) and json files broken into dates.
  2. Select your zip using the "Choose File" button in the form below.
  3. The app will process your zip and once it is complete these instructions will be replaced by a list of your channels that you can click on. Within each of those will be the concatenated chat history from each day that was available in the export.
